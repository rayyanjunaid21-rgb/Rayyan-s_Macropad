## BOM
For this project, I would need:
- 6 Through-hole 1N4148 Diodes
- 6 MX-Style switches
- 1 EC11 Rotary encoder
- 6 Blank DSA keycaps
- 4 M3x16mm screws
- 4 M3x5mx4mm heatset inserts
- Soldering Iron
